About video

An impressive Guide Video on how to gain attached mucosa around implants using the Kazanjian Technique. Attached mucosa has an important influence on peri-implant health around dental implants. Proven in the latest literature reviews, fixed mucosa is more important than keratinized, which places great value on the Kazanjan. Here you will learn how to plan your surgery, where to perform incisions, how to manage different soft tissue layers, separate muscles from periosteum, and finally how to repair and suture tissues in newly modified conditions, but in contrast, these are videos for you as academic manuals for your daily surgeries.

All of these materials will be available to watch at any time and to use for your academic goals. Absolute Guide Video on how to perform immediate implantation in an aesthetic zone and how to avoid all general mistakes. Here you will learn how to clinically investigate your patient. I will then show you how to interpret patient CCT data and according to these data, decisively choose between an immediate implant procedure OR a 4-6 week postponement, including how to plan the implant positioning. Here you will be also instructed on how to extract front teeth without damaging socket structure.
